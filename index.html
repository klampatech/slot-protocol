HOW TO PLAY

1. Click on the drop zone to release a ball.

2. Balls bounce off pegs - hit as many as you can!

3. Fill the progress bar to clear the floor.

4. Chain hits for combo multipliers (x7 max).

5. Collect slots at the bottom for bonus rewards.

6. Use payloads for special abilities.

7. Don't lose all balls before clearing the floor!
